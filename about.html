About IntraSync Industrial

Pioneering the Future of Precast Manufacturing

At IntraSync Industrial, we're transforming how readymix concrete and aggregates companies operate through cutting-edge technology and industry expertise. Our mission is simple yet ambitious: to unlock your plant's true potential with data-driven production scheduling and AI-powered optimization tools.

Leadership That Delivers Results

Zachary Frye - Chief Technology Officer & Founder

Zachary Frye founded IntraSync Industrial with a vision to revolutionize the readymix and aggregates industry through innovative technology solutions. With a foundation in Mechanical Engineering from California State University-Sacramento and specialized expertise in Graphic Design from Sierra College, Zach brings a unique blend of technical depth and design thinking to industrial software development.

Engineering Excellence in Action

Over the past seven years, Zach has led the development and refinement of the IntraSync AggLogic platform, pioneering advanced AI solutions that have set new industry benchmarks in process optimization. His hands-on approach to solving real-world readymix and aggregates production challenges has resulted in:

  • Significant Cost Efficiencies: Clients achieve an average of 20% time savings on manual scheduling and coordination tasks.
  • Industry-Leading Innovation: Development of nine integrated modules spanning the entire readymix and aggregates lifecycle from engineering to installation.
  • Technical Leadership: Successfully architected a comprehensive software stack including ExpressJS APIs, ReactJS Progressive Web Applications, and custom AI integration tools.
  • Operational Excellence: Delivered measurable improvements including up to 15% reduction in site crane time and 5-10% fuel cost savings through optimized delivery routing.

Technology Leadership & Innovation

Under Zach's technical leadership, IntraSync has built a comprehensive technology platform featuring:

  • Modern Software Solutions: Development of integrated web and mobile applications that work seamlessly across all devices and platforms.
  • Intelligent Integration: Custom solutions that connect engineering tools like Revit with production systems for seamless data flow.
  • AI-Powered Capabilities: Advanced Machine Learning implementation for predictive maintenance, demand forecasting, and automated quality control.
  • Team Development: Successfully leads and mentors a development team to create unified systems that enhance communication and coordination across all departments.

Our Industry Impact

Born from solving real production challenges faced by commercial readymix and aggregates companies in California, IntraSync Industrial has evolved into a comprehensive solution provider serving both architectural high-end custom projects and utility high-repetition products. Our platform transforms complex data into optimized schedules, real-time insights, and streamlined workflows that deliver measurable ROI.
