About IntraSync Industrial

Pioneering the Future of Readymix & Aggregates

At IntraSync Industrial, we're transforming how readymix concrete and aggregate producers operate through cutting-edge technology and industry expertise. Leadership That Delivers Results

Zachary Frye - Chief Technology Officer & Founder

Zachary Frye founded IntraSync Industrial with a vision to revolutionize the readymix and materials industry through innovative technology solutions. With a foundation in Mechanical Engineering from California State University-Sacramento and specialized expertise in Graphic Design from Sierra College, Zach brings a unique blend of technical depth and design thinking to industrial software development.

Engineering Excellence in Action

Over the past seven years, Zach has led the development and refinement of the IntraSync MaterialsLogic platform, pioneering advanced AI solutions that have set new industry benchmarks in readymix and material operations. His hands-on approach to solving real-world production challenges has resulted in breakthrough innovations:

  • Revolutionary Technology: First-to-market drone quarry scanning delivering 90% faster surveys with 1% accuracy, transforming inventory management.
  • AI Quality Control: Instant photographic aggregate analysis providing lab-quality results in seconds with 98% accuracy, eliminating hours of manual testing.
  • Significant Efficiency Gains: MaterialsLogic is designed to deliver 25% efficiency improvements and 30% waste reduction through precision control and real-time optimization.
  • Complete Platform Integration: Unified financial, operational, and quality data management with AI-powered insights and predictive analytics.
  • Technical Innovation: Successfully architected comprehensive software stack including computer vision AI, drone photogrammetry, cloud analytics, and mobile control applications.

Technology Leadership & Innovation

Under Zach's technical leadership, IntraSync has built a groundbreaking technology platform featuring:

  • Drone Integration: Automated aerial surveying technology for real-time quarry mapping, volume calculations, and 3D terrain modeling.
  • Computer Vision AI: Advanced image recognition for instant aggregate gradation, particle shape analysis, and quality assessment.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Live batch tracking with customizable alerts, automated test forms, and compliance documentation.
  • Intelligent Mix Design: AI-powered optimization for cost and CO2 reduction with performance-based recommendations.
  • Multi-Plant Analytics: Cloud-based dashboards providing unified visibility across all operations with predictive insights.
  • Mobile-First Architecture: Native iOS/Android applications enabling remote control and monitoring from anywhere.
