Built for Readymix & Aggregate Operations

Dispatch challenges unique to concrete and aggregate delivery require specialized solutions.

Readymix Drum Monitoring: Track drum rotation time, slump values, water additions, and concrete temperature from batching through delivery. Automated alerts prevent out-of-spec deliveries.
Aggregate Multi-Material Loads: Manage complex loads with multiple aggregate types (sand, gravel, crushed stone) with automatic tonnage allocation and separate pricing per material.
Weight Ticket Integration: Direct integration with truck scales for accurate tonnage capture. Automatic variance detection between ordered and delivered quantities with reconciliation workflows.
Slump & Temperature Tracking: Real-time monitoring of concrete properties from batch plant through delivery. Automatic documentation on delivery tickets with trend analysis and compliance reporting.
Delivery Window Optimization: Coordinate concrete pours with contractor schedules and optimize aggregate deliveries to minimize truck queue times at busy construction sites.
Quarry-to-Site Optimization: Intelligent routing from multiple quarry locations to delivery sites, factoring in material availability, haul distance, and traffic patterns for optimal fleet utilization.
