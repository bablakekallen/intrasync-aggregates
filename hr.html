Built for Aggregates & Ready-Mix Workforce Needs

Generic HR systems don't understand the complexity of heavy construction materials operations. AggLogic HR handles the unique challenges of aggregates and ready-mix workforces.

Equipment Operator Certifications

  • Heavy Equipment Qualifications: Track loader, excavator, dozer, and haul truck operator certifications with expiration alerts
  • Plant Equipment Certifications: Crusher, conveyor, screening, and batching plant operator qualifications
  • Mobile Equipment: Forklift, skid steer, telehandler, and other mobile equipment certifications

CDL & DOT Compliance Tracking

  • CDL License Management: Track Class A/B/C CDL licenses, endorsements (tanker, hazmat), and expiration dates
  • DOT Medical Certifications: Automatic alerts for medical card expirations and renewal requirements
  • Hours of Service Compliance: Integration with ELD systems for driver hour tracking and compliance reporting

MSHA Compliance & Safety

  • Part 46 Training: New miner training, annual refresher training, hazard training, and task training tracking
  • Training Documentation: Complete 5000-23 form generation and record keeping for MSHA compliance
  • Incident Reporting: Digital 7000-1 accident/injury/illness reporting integrated with employee records

Certified Payroll & Prevailing Wage

  • Davis-Bacon Compliance: Automatic WH-347 certified payroll report generation for federal government jobs
  • State Prevailing Wage: Manage state-specific prevailing wage rates and compliance reporting by project and locality
  • Fringe Benefits Tracking: Separate tracking of base wages and fringe benefits for government contract compliance
