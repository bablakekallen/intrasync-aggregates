MaterialsLogic QC Features

Capture, analyze, and certify aggregate quality with AI-powered automation

AI Gradation Analysis

Instant photographic analysis of aggregate gradation with 98% accuracy. Get lab-quality results in seconds, not hours. Automated ASTM C136 compliance.

Moisture Monitoring

Real-time aggregate moisture content tracking. Data flows directly to BatchLogic for automatic water compensation in mix designs.

Material Certifications

Digital certification management for all aggregate sources. Track supplier quality scores, expiration dates, and compliance status in one place.

ASTM Compliance Testing

Automated test workflows for ASTM C33, C136, C117, C127/128, and more. Digital forms with automatic calculations and pass/fail indicators.

Supplier Scorecards

Track supplier quality performance over time. Automated scorecards based on test results, delivery consistency, and certification compliance.

Drone Inventory QC

Aerial stockpile scanning validates inventory accuracy within 1%. Detect contamination, segregation, and quality issues before they impact production.

Real-Time Alerts

Instant notifications when aggregate quality deviates from specifications. Alerts flow to batch operators, QC managers, and purchasing teams.

Full Traceability

Track any aggregate lot from quarry to finished product. Complete audit trail for DOT projects, PCI compliance, and customer requirements.

CastLogic ERP Module

CastLogic Verify

Your aggregate QC data becomes even more powerful when combined with CastLogic's comprehensive quality management system

AI Computer Vision Inspections

Automated defect detection using computer vision. Dimensional measurements, surface finish analysis, and instant pass/fail results linked back to aggregate source data.

Defect Tracking & NCRs

Non-conformance logging with photo/video documentation. Root cause analysis traces defects back to aggregate quality, batch parameters, or production issues.

PCI Compliance Management

Built-in PCI certification templates. Aggregate certifications, batch records, and inspection results combine for complete audit documentation.

Concrete Strength Testing

7-day, 14-day, and 28-day cylinder tracking. Correlate strength results with aggregate gradation and batch parameters for continuous improvement.

Digital QC Checklists

Custom inspection templates with conditional logic. Mobile app for field use, electronic signatures, and automatic data sync to quality records.

Complete Audit Trail

Full traceability from aggregate source through batch production to delivered precast piece. Timestamped logs, material batch tracking, and digital record retention.
