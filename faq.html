Everything you need to know about AggLogic's AI-powered platform for readymix and aggregates production

What is AggLogic?

AggLogic is the first AI-powered operating system specifically designed for readymix concrete and aggregates producers. It combines cutting-edge drone quarry scanning (90% faster surveys, 1% accuracy), AI photographic aggregate analysis (98% accuracy, instant results), real-time load monitoring, intelligent mix design, financial data management, and multi-plant operations management. AggLogic helps producers achieve 25% efficiency gains with zero upfront fees.

How does drone scanning work?

AggLogic Drone Scanning uses advanced aerial surveying technology to map your quarry in real-time. The system captures high-resolution aerial imagery, processes it with AI algorithms, and generates accurate 3D models of your quarry. This provides real-time volume calculations, inventory tracking, and detailed mapping. Surveys that traditionally took days can now be completed in hours, reducing survey time by 90% while improving accuracy to within 1%. Perfect for monitoring stockpiles, planning extractions, and managing inventory.

What is photographic aggregate analysis?

AggLogic's AI photographic aggregate analysis uses computer vision and machine learning to instantly analyze aggregate samples. Simply photograph your aggregate with a smartphone, and the system provides lab-quality results in seconds including gradation analysis, shape classification, and quality metrics. This technology achieves 98% accuracy compared to manual lab testing, eliminates the 2-4 hour wait time for lab results, and costs significantly less. Perfect for quality control, incoming material verification, and production monitoring.
