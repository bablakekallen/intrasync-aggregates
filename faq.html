Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AggLogic readymix and aggregates production software

What is AggLogic?

AggLogic is comprehensive production management software specifically designed for readymix concrete and aggregates manufacturers. It includes nine integrated modules covering AI-powered scheduling, real-time inventory tracking, quality control automation, BIM/CAD integration, delivery management, and predictive analytics. The platform increases production efficiency by an average of 20% and is trusted by leading manufacturers.

How is AggLogic priced?

AggLogic pricing is customized based on plant size, number of users, and the specific modules you select. This modular approach ensures you only pay for what you need. Pricing is structured as an annual subscription that includes all selected modules, full implementation, team training, and ongoing support. The average ROI is achieved in two months or less from implementation through significant efficiency gains. Contact us for a personalized quote and ROI calculation.

How long does AggLogic implementation take?

Typical implementation takes 6-8 weeks from kickoff to full deployment. Week 1-2: System configuration and data import. Week 3-4: Team training and parallel testing with existing systems. Week 5-6: Go-live with core modules. Week 7-8: Optimization and activation of advanced features. Most clients see measurable improvements within the first 30 days. We provide dedicated implementation support throughout the entire process.

Does AggLogic integrate with BIM and CAD software?

Yes. AggLogic integrates directly with Tekla Structures, Revit, AutoCAD, and other major BIM/CAD platforms through the AggLogic Engage module. The system can import project data, create digital twins of products, and sync changes bidirectionally. This eliminates manual data entry and ensures production always works from the latest designs. We also support custom integrations for other engineering tools.

What results can I expect from using AggLogic?

Based on numerous successful implementations, clients typically achieve: 20% average efficiency improvement, 10.2% revenue increase through improved throughput, 75% reduction in scheduling time, 30% decrease in rework and quality issues, 98% on-time delivery rate, and positive ROI in two months or less from implementation. Results vary based on current processes, plant size, and level of adoption. We provide ROI projections during the evaluation phase.

Can AggLogic work with my existing ERP system?

Yes. AggLogic provides a comprehensive REST API that integrates with most ERP, accounting, and business management systems. We can sync customer data, orders, inventory, production status, and financial information bidirectionally. Common integrations include SAP, Microsoft Dynamics, QuickBooks, and custom enterprise systems. Our integration team provides full support during setup and ongoing maintenance.

What makes AggLogic different from generic manufacturing software?

AggLogic is built specifically for readymix concrete and aggregates production with deep understanding of industry workflows. It handles mold management, cure time optimization, pour scheduling, lifting calculations, yard organization with GPS mapping, and delivery sequencing out of the box. Generic ERP systems require 6-12 months of extensive customization to handle these readymix and aggregates-specific workflows, while AggLogic works immediately with minimal configuration. Our team has decades of readymix and aggregates industry experience.

What size plants is AggLogic designed for?

AggLogic scales from small plants producing 500 pieces annually to large facilities producing 50,000+ pieces. Our modular approach allows you to build a package with only the features you need, ensuring a cost-effective solution for any size operation.

How does the AI scheduling work?

AggLogic Schedule uses machine learning algorithms to optimize production schedules based on mold availability, cure time requirements, resource constraints, delivery deadlines, and historical performance data. The AI learns from your plant's patterns and continuously improves recommendations. It can generate optimized schedules in minutes that would take schedulers 20+ hours manually, while considering hundreds of variables simultaneously. The system also provides what-if scenario planning for capacity analysis.

What is computer vision quality control?

AggLogic Verify uses computer vision cameras and AI to automatically detect defects during production. The system can identify surface imperfections, dimensional inconsistencies, color variations, crack formations, and aggregate distribution issues in real-time. It processes each panel in 2-3 seconds versus 8-15 minutes for manual inspection. The AI has been trained on 10,000+ readymix and aggregates products and achieves 99% accuracy in defect detection, catching issues 90% earlier in the production process before they become costly reworks.

Does AggLogic have mobile apps?

Yes. AggLogic includes native mobile apps for iOS and Android, plus Progressive Web Apps that work on any device. Mobile apps are included in your subscription at no additional cost. Field crews can access production schedules, update job status, capture photos, report issues, and view yard maps from tablets or smartphones. The apps work offline and sync automatically when connectivity is restored. All modules have mobile-optimized interfaces.

How secure is my data in AggLogic?

AggLogic uses enterprise-grade security with data encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance and conduct annual third-party security audits. Data is hosted in secure AWS data centers with automatic backups every 6 hours, 99.9% uptime SLA, and disaster recovery protocols. You maintain full ownership of your data and can export it at any time. We never share customer data with third parties.

Can AggLogic handle both architectural and utility products?

Yes. AggLogic is designed to handle the full spectrum of readymix and aggregates products. For architectural/custom work, it manages complex BIM models, unique specifications, custom molds, and multi-component deliveries with precise sequencing. For utility/repetitive products, it optimizes high-volume production lines, standardized workflows, and bulk deliveries. Many plants produce both types and use AggLogic to manage their entire operation with a single unified system.
