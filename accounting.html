AggLogic Accounting

Complete Financial Management for Readymix & Aggregates

Full ERP accounting integrated with your operations. From ticket to invoice to financial statement—all in one unified platform.

Operations-to-Financial Integration

Unlike standalone accounting software, AggLogic Accounting connects directly to your plant operations—tickets generate invoices, drone inventory valuations flow to your balance sheet, and every load is tracked from quarry to payment.

Standalone Accounting Software

  • • Manual ticket entry creates delays and errors
  • • Inventory valuations don't match drone data
  • • Quality costs tracked in separate systems
  • • No real-time visibility into per-load profitability
  • • Month-end requires reconciling multiple systems

AggLogic Integrated Accounting

  • ✓ Automatic invoicing from tickets—zero manual entry
  • ✓ Drone inventory valuation synced directly to GL
  • ✓ Quality costs tracked per batch and project
  • ✓ Per-load profitability visible in real-time
  • ✓ Single source of truth for all financial data

Unique AggLogic Financial Features

Drone-scanned stockpile volumes automatically calculate inventory valuations and post to your general ledger. No more manual inventory counts or guesswork.

  • ✓ Real-time inventory valuation
  • ✓ Automatic variance detection
  • ✓ Historical tracking and trending

Quality Cost Tracking

AI aggregate analysis results flow directly to quality cost tracking. Know exactly how much quality issues are costing you per project or customer.
