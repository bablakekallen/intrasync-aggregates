The Power of Fully Integrated ERP Accounting

Unlike standalone accounting software, AggLogic Accounting is the financial backbone of your complete ERP system—seamlessly connecting quarry operations, batch plants, dispatch, inventory, and HR into one unified platform.

❌ Standalone Accounting Software

  • • Manual ticket entry from scale house creates errors and delays

  • ✓ Automatic data flow from scale tickets to invoices—zero manual entry

  • ✓ Royalty tracking integrated with production volumes and financial reporting
  • ✓ Live profitability dashboards by material, customer, project, or location
  • ✓ Month-end close in hours not days with automated reconciliations

AggLogic Accounting isn't just software—it's the financial intelligence layer that connects your entire operation, eliminating data silos and giving you complete visibility from production to profit.

Built for Aggregates & Readymix Operations

Generic accounting software doesn't understand your business. AggLogic Accounting is purpose-built for the unique financial requirements of aggregates and readymix operations.

Aggregates-Specific Features

  • Cost Per Ton Analytics: Real-time tracking of production, processing, and delivery costs per ton by material type
  • Royalty & Lease Tracking: Automated calculation and accrual of royalties based on extracted tonnage with payment schedules
  • Equipment Cost Allocation: Track crusher, loader, and processing equipment costs allocated to specific materials
  • Drone Inventory Integration: Automated stockpile valuations from drone surveys posted directly to GL
  • Reclamation Cost Accruals: Track and accrue future site reclamation and restoration obligations

Readymix-Specific Features

  • Cost Per Yard Tracking: Complete batch-level costing including materials, labor, equipment, and delivery
  • Batch Ticket Integration: Every batch automatically creates cost entries and links to customer invoices
  • Fuel Surcharge Automation: Dynamic fuel surcharge calculation and application based on current diesel prices
  • Mix Design Profitability: Analyze profitability by mix design, strength class, and admixture usage
  • Short Load & Overage Billing: Automated billing adjustments for actual vs. ordered quantities
