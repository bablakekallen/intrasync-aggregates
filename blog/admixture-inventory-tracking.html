Admixture Management

Admixtures are essential for modern concrete performance, but their inventory management presents unique challenges—shelf life limits, multiple product types, and precise dosage requirements.

The Complexity of Admixture Management

Most concrete producers use 5-15 different admixtures across their operations: water reducers, air entrainers, accelerators, retarders, and specialty products. Each has different storage requirements, shelf lives, and usage patterns.

Key Management Challenges

  • Tracking multiple products across multiple tanks and totes
  • Managing shelf life and FIFO rotation
  • Reconciling actual usage with batch system dispensing data
  • Coordinating reorders with production schedules
  • Maintaining proper storage temperatures

Industry Insight

Producers with automated admixture tracking report 50% reduction in emergency orders and eliminate expired product waste. Integration with batch systems enables automatic inventory deduction with each batch.

Modern Tracking Solutions

Tank-level sensors, flow meters, and batch system integration provide real-time visibility into admixture inventory. Smart algorithms predict usage and generate reorder alerts accounting for lead times and minimum order quantities.
