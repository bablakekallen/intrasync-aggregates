Unified Quality Control Across Your Entire Concrete Operation

By IntraSync Industrial • January 5, 2025 • 15 min read

Quality problems in concrete are rarely isolated incidents. A gradation shift in the quarry affects mix workability at the batch plant, which impacts placement at the job site, which shows up as a strength failure weeks later. Traditional QC systems—spreadsheets, siloed software, paper forms—can't trace these connections. IntraSync's integrated platform can.

The Quality Control Fragmentation Problem

Walk through a typical concrete operation and you'll find quality data scattered everywhere:

  • Quarry: Gradation data in one system (or paper files)
  • Batch plant: Batch weights and recipes in the controller
  • Lab: Cylinder breaks and test results in another database
  • Dispatch: Delivery information in yet another system
  • Field: Slump tests and placement notes often on paper

When a quality issue arises, connecting these data points requires manual detective work—if it's possible at all. By the time you trace the root cause, the problem has multiplied across many deliveries.

The IntraSync Approach: One Platform, Complete Traceability

IntraSync Industrial's platform family—CastLogic, AggLogic, and BatchLogic—share a common data foundation. Quality data flows automatically between systems, creating complete traceability without manual intervention.

From Quarry to Delivery

Complete Quality Chain

  1. Quarry extraction: Drone scanning tracks which face materials came from
  2. Stockpile: AI aggregate analysis captures gradation as material enters storage
  3. Batching: BatchLogic records exact proportions used, adjusted for material variability
  4. Delivery: GPS tracking links tickets to exact batches
  5. Testing: Field and lab results tied to specific loads
  6. Analysis: AI connects outcomes to inputs, identifying patterns

Quality Control Features Across the Platform

AggLogic: Aggregate Quality

Quality control starts with raw materials. AggLogic provides:

  • AI photographic analysis: Instant gradation results with 98% accuracy
  • Continuous monitoring: Test every load instead of occasional samples
  • Trend tracking: Identify gradual shifts before they become problems
  • Supplier scorecards: Track quality performance by source
  • Specification compliance: Automatic alerts when materials drift out of spec

BatchLogic: Production Quality

At the batch plant, BatchLogic ensures consistent production:

  • Recipe optimization: Adjust for incoming material variability
  • Batch accuracy: Monitor weighing precision across all materials
  • Mix time control: Ensure adequate mixing for uniform distribution
  • Water management: Track moisture compensation and w/c ratio
  • Predictive strength: AI estimates 28-day strength from batch data

CastLogic: Precast Quality

For precast operations, CastLogic adds:

  • Mold tracking: Associate products with specific pour events
  • Curing monitoring: Temperature and humidity tracking
  • Dimensional inspection: Verify finished products meet specifications
  • Certification management: Track certifications and compliance requirements

Automated QC Workflows

The platform automates quality control workflows that traditionally require manual coordination:

Test Scheduling

Based on production volume, material sources, and specification requirements, the system automatically schedules required tests. QC technicians see exactly what needs testing each day.

Data Collection

Mobile apps guide technicians through standardized testing procedures. Data flows directly into the central system—no transcription errors, no paper filing.

Automatic Alerts

When test results fall outside acceptable ranges, the system immediately notifies relevant personnel. For critical failures, production can be automatically paused pending review.

Root Cause Analysis

When issues occur, AI-powered analysis correlates outcomes with all upstream factors—material sources, batch data, environmental conditions, equipment parameters—to identify likely causes.
