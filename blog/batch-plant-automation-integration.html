Complete Batch Plant Automation: Integrating Hardware with Intelligence

By IntraSync Industrial • January 8, 2025 • 12 min read

Batch plant operators face a challenging decision: continue running aging control systems that work but lack modern capabilities, or undergo expensive rip-and-replace upgrades. BatchLogic offers a third path—keeping your proven hardware while adding AI-powered intelligence on top.

The Batch Plant Technology Gap

Most ready-mix operations run batch plants with control systems installed years or even decades ago. These systems work—they weigh materials, control gates and conveyors, and produce concrete. But they lack the connectivity and intelligence that modern operations demand:

  • Siloed data: Batch data stays locked in the controller, inaccessible to management systems
  • Manual ticketing: Operators manually enter ticket information, creating delays and errors
  • No optimization: Systems batch the recipe exactly as entered, without intelligence about material variability or cost optimization
  • Limited visibility: Plant managers can't see real-time production status remotely
  • Difficult reporting: Generating production reports requires manual data extraction and manipulation

Traditional solutions require replacing entire control systems—a massive capital investment that also means retraining staff and risking production disruptions during transition.

BatchLogic: Intelligence Without Replacement

BatchLogic takes a fundamentally different approach. Instead of replacing your batch controller, we connect to it—extracting data, adding intelligence, and sending optimized recipes back. Your operators keep using familiar interfaces while gaining powerful new capabilities.

Universal Hardware Compatibility

BatchLogic connects to batch controllers from all major manufacturers:

  • Command Alkon COMMANDbatch and COMMANDassurance
  • Marcotte Systems
  • Standley Batch Systems
  • Sysdyne Concrete Ready Series
  • Custom PLC-based systems
  • Legacy systems with serial or Ethernet connectivity

How Integration Works

BatchLogic uses multiple integration methods depending on your existing infrastructure:

Integration Options

  • Direct API: Modern systems with REST or SOAP interfaces connect directly
  • Database sync: BatchLogic reads from and writes to existing database tables
  • Protocol bridges: OPC-UA, Modbus, and other industrial protocols supported
  • File exchange: Legacy systems can use automated file import/export
  • Edge devices: For older systems, we install small edge computers that bridge to modern connectivity

AI-Powered Batch Optimization

Once connected, BatchLogic adds intelligence that standalone controllers can't provide:

Real-Time Mix Adjustment

When aggregate gradation varies (detected by AggLogic's photographic analysis), BatchLogic automatically adjusts proportions to maintain target concrete properties. The batch controller receives an optimized recipe, not just the standard design.

Cost & CO2 Optimization

For every batch, BatchLogic evaluates material costs and carbon impact, suggesting optimizations that maintain performance while reducing expense. Generate unlimited cost-optimized mix variations without manual engineering time.

Predictive Quality Control

By analyzing historical batch data alongside test results, BatchLogic predicts concrete strength before cylinders break. Catch potential issues while material is still in the plant, not days later in the lab.

Unified Ticketing & Dispatch

BatchLogic transforms ticketing from a bottleneck into an automated flow:

  • Automatic ticket generation: Orders from dispatch systems flow directly to batch controllers
  • Live ticket tracking: Every truck's status visible in real-time with GPS integration
  • Customer notifications: Automated SMS/email updates keep customers informed
  • Digital signatures: Capture delivery confirmation on driver mobile devices
  • Instant invoicing: Tickets automatically generate invoices in your accounting system

Multi-Plant Visibility

For operations with multiple plants, BatchLogic provides unified visibility:

  • Centralized dashboard: Monitor all plants from a single interface
  • Standardized recipes: Push mix designs to all plants simultaneously
  • Performance benchmarking: Compare efficiency metrics across locations
  • Load balancing: Route orders to optimize fleet utilization across plants

Integration with the IntraSync Platform

BatchLogic is part of the IntraSync Industrial platform family, enabling seamless data flow:

  • AggLogic: Aggregate quality data automatically informs batch adjustments
  • CastLogic: Precast production scheduling integrates with batch planning
  • Quality Control: All test results, batch data, and certifications in one system
  • Financial: Complete cost tracking from material receipt through delivery

Implementation Without Disruption

We understand that production can't stop for software upgrades. BatchLogic implementations are designed to minimize disruption:

  1. Assessment: We evaluate your existing systems and design integration approach
  2. Parallel installation: BatchLogic connects alongside existing systems, not replacing them
  3. Shadow mode: System runs in parallel, validating data without affecting production
  4. Phased activation: Features enabled gradually as operators gain confidence
  5. Continuous optimization: AI models improve over time with your production data
