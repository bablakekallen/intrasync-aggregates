Real-Time Load Monitoring: Visibility from Batch to Delivery

By IntraSync Industrial • January 12, 2025 • 8 min read

In ready-mix concrete, every minute matters. Concrete is a perishable product—once it leaves the plant, the clock starts ticking. Real-time load monitoring gives you visibility into every truck, every batch, and every delivery, enabling proactive management instead of reactive firefighting.

The Visibility Gap

Traditional ready-mix operations suffer from visibility gaps at critical moments:

  • Where's the truck? Dispatchers rely on driver calls or manual check-ins
  • Is the load on time? Customers wait anxiously without updates
  • What's the drum status? Concrete age and condition unknown until arrival
  • Was the batch correct? Quality issues discovered at the job site
  • Who signed for it? Paper tickets lost or damaged

These gaps lead to customer frustration, quality issues, and operational inefficiency. Dispatchers spend time chasing information instead of optimizing fleet utilization.

AggLogic Real-Time Monitoring

AggLogic's load monitoring module provides complete visibility into every aspect of the delivery process:

Live Fleet Tracking

GPS-enabled tracking shows every truck's location in real-time on an interactive map. See routes, current positions, and estimated arrival times for all active deliveries. Geofencing alerts notify dispatchers when trucks enter or leave designated zones.

Batch Data Integration

When combined with BatchLogic, load monitoring includes complete batch information:

  • Exact material weights and proportions
  • Mix design and specification compliance
  • Batch time and plant of origin
  • Predicted strength based on actual batch data

Concrete Age Tracking

The system tracks elapsed time from batch to delivery, alerting dispatchers when loads approach time limits. Temperature sensors (where equipped) provide additional data for hot weather operations.

Proactive Alerts

  • Time warnings: Alerts when loads approach specification time limits
  • Route deviations: Notifications when trucks leave expected routes
  • Delay detection: Automatic identification of unusual wait times
  • Quality flags: Alerts for any batch data outside normal ranges

Customer Communication

Keep customers informed automatically throughout the delivery process:

Automated Notifications

  • Order confirmation: Immediate notification when order is scheduled
  • Dispatch alert: Notification when truck leaves the plant
  • ETA updates: Real-time arrival estimates based on traffic
  • Arrival notice: Alert when truck arrives at job site
  • Delivery confirmation: Digital receipt with all batch details

Customer Portal

Give customers self-service access to track their own deliveries. They can see truck locations, review past tickets, and access quality documentation—without calling your office.

Digital Ticketing

Replace paper tickets with digital documentation that's always accessible:

  • Electronic tickets: Generated automatically from batch data
  • Digital signatures: Capture customer sign-off on driver mobile devices
  • Photo documentation: Attach delivery photos to tickets
  • Instant availability: Tickets accessible immediately from any device
  • Automatic invoicing: Tickets flow directly to billing systems

Quality Control Integration

Real-time monitoring connects with IntraSync's unified quality control system:

  • Batch traceability: Link every delivery to specific batches and materials
  • Test scheduling: Automatically schedule required tests based on deliveries
  • Field data capture: Record slump tests and placement conditions
  • Issue tracking: Flag and investigate any quality concerns

Dispatcher Tools

Dispatchers gain powerful tools to optimize operations:

  • Visual dispatch board: See all orders, trucks, and deliveries at a glance
  • Drag-and-drop scheduling: Easily assign and reassign trucks
  • Load optimization: Suggestions for efficient truck utilization
  • Exception management: Focus attention on problems, not routine operations

Results

Operations using AggLogic real-time monitoring report:

Measured Improvements

  • 25% improvement in fleet utilization through better dispatch
  • 40% reduction in customer service calls
  • 90% decrease in lost or disputed tickets
  • 15% improvement in on-time delivery performance
  • Zero surprises on concrete age or quality issues
