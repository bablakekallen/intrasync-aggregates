Asphalt Binder Management: Storage, Temperature & Quality Control

December 2024 • 8 min read

Binder Management

Asphalt binder is the glue that holds pavement together. Proper management of binder inventory, temperature, and quality is essential for producing high-performance asphalt mixes.

Critical Binder Management Factors

Asphalt binder requires careful temperature control, proper tank management, and quality verification. Overheating degrades the binder, while underheating prevents proper mixing with aggregates.

Key Management Requirements

  • Temperature monitoring and control (typically 275-325°F)
  • Tank level monitoring and inventory tracking
  • PG grade verification and documentation
  • Polymer-modified binder handling requirements
  • Heating time limits to prevent degradation

Industry Insight

Plants with automated binder temperature monitoring report 20% reduction in energy costs and virtually eliminate degraded binder waste. Real-time alerts prevent both overheating and production delays from underheated material.
