Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about AggLogic modules and features

Can I choose which modules to enable?

Yes! AggLogic is modular. You can start with the core modules you need most and activate additional modules as your operation grows. Most customers start with Real-Time Load Monitoring and Multi-Plant Dashboards, then expand to AI features.

How long does implementation take?

We handle all data migration and integration at no cost. Most plants go live with core modules in 2-4 weeks. AI features are typically activated within 30 days. We provide dedicated support throughout implementation and beyond.

Do the modules integrate with existing systems?

Absolutely. AggLogic integrates with most ERP systems, accounting software, and batch plant equipment. Our team will handle all integration work as part of implementation. No additional fees for standard integrations.

What kind of ROI can I expect?

Most customers see measurable ROI within 60 days. Typical savings include 15-20% cost reduction from mix optimization, 25% efficiency gains from automation, and significant rework cost reduction from quality improvements. Drone scanning typically pays for itself in 3 months.

Is training included?

Yes, comprehensive training is included at no extra cost. We provide on-site training at your facility, online training sessions, and ongoing support. Unlimited users means everyone at your operation can access the system with appropriate permissions.
