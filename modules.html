Real-Time Load Monitoring

Track every single batch from production to delivery with complete visibility. Real-time alerts notify your team of any issues before they become problems. Automated test forms ensure consistent quality standards across all loads, with live compliance tracking and audit trails built in.

  • Live batch tracking with GPS integration
  • Customizable alerts for quality metrics
  • Automated test form collection
  • Compliance tracking & audit trails

AI Mix Design Optimization

Generate unlimited mix designs with automatic cost and CO2 optimization. AI-powered recommendations analyze your historical performance data and current conditions to suggest the best designs. Reduce costs while improving environmental impact and concrete performance.

  • Unlimited mix generation
  • Automatic cost optimization
  • Carbon footprint reduction
  • Performance-based recommendations

Automated Trial Batching

Streamline your mix development process with automated trial batching workflows. Track performance metrics across test batches, compare results side-by-side, and get optimization recommendations to accelerate your development timeline.

  • Automated workflow management
  • Performance tracking across batches
  • Side-by-side result comparisons
  • Optimization recommendations

Live Ticket Tracking

Achieve complete transparency from batch production through delivery. Real-time GPS tracking shows exactly where every load is, while customers receive automatic notifications. Reduce delivery issues and improve customer satisfaction with visibility.

  • Real-time GPS integration
  • Batch-to-delivery visibility
  • Customer notifications
  • Delivery proof & sign-off

Multi-Plant Dashboards

Manage your entire operation—multiple plants, quarries, and dispatch centers—from a single unified dashboard. Standardize operations across locations, benchmark performance, share best practices, and make enterprise-wide decisions with confidence.

  • Unified multi-location management
  • Cross-location performance benchmarking
  • Best practice sharing
  • Centralized reporting

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about MaterialsLogic modules and features

Can I choose which modules to enable?

Yes! MaterialsLogic is modular. You can start with the core modules you need most and activate additional modules as your operation grows. Most customers start with Real-Time Load Monitoring and Multi-Plant Dashboards, then expand to AI features.

How long does implementation take?

We handle all data migration and integration at no cost. Most plants go live with core modules in 2-4 weeks. AI features are typically activated within 30 days. We provide dedicated support throughout implementation and beyond.

Do the modules integrate with existing systems?

Absolutely. MaterialsLogic integrates with most ERP systems, accounting software, and batch plant equipment. Our team will handle all integration work as part of implementation. No additional fees for standard integrations.

What kind of ROI can I expect?

MaterialsLogic is designed to deliver measurable ROI within 60 days. Expected savings include 15-20% cost reduction from mix optimization, 25% efficiency gains from automation, and significant rework cost reduction from quality improvements. Drone scanning is designed to pay for itself within 3 months.

Is training included?

Yes, comprehensive training is included at no extra cost. We provide on-site training at your facility, online training sessions, and ongoing support. Unlimited users means everyone at your operation can access the system with appropriate permissions.
